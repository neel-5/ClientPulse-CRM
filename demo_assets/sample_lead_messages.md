# Sample Lead Messages

Use these only in demo mode.

1. **Clinic:** “Hi, we receive about 40 enquiries a week on WhatsApp. Can this remind our
   receptionist which patients still need a callback?”
2. **Coaching:** “We have four counsellors and lose track when a student messages a second
   time. Can everyone see the same lead stage?”
3. **Agency:** “Can your dashboard show which source gives us the most won projects?”
4. **Freelancer:** “I need a simple way to follow up without maintaining three spreadsheets.”
5. **Local service:** “Can I export everything to the Google Sheet my owner already checks?”
