# Sample Business Use Case: Bloom Skin Clinic

This is a fictional demonstration, not a real customer claim.

Bloom Skin Clinic receives enquiries from WhatsApp and Google Ads. The receptionist replies,
but follow-up ownership and callback dates are inconsistent. The owner manually counts
enquiries in a spreadsheet at the end of each week.

ClientPulse demo:

1. A mock WhatsApp message creates a lead.
2. The receptionist qualifies it and assigns a value.
3. AI drafts a respectful appointment reply.
4. The lead moves from New to Interested.
5. A callback is scheduled and appears in the daily agenda.
6. The owner refreshes the Google Sheets dashboard to view source conversion and overdue
   follow-ups.

Success criteria for a real clinic pilot would be fewer missed callbacks, faster response,
and a weekly report that does not require manual message counting.
