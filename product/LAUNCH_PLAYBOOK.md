# ClientPulse Launch Playbook

## Goal

Onboard 3-5 real small-business users, observe usage for at least one week, and collect
honest evidence of repeated use and product changes.

## Ideal first users

- A coaching institute with 2-5 counsellors.
- A clinic receiving enquiries on WhatsApp.
- A freelancer or agency handling 20+ monthly leads.
- A local service business already maintaining a Google Sheet.

Choose users you can support directly. Do not start with regulated or highly sensitive data.

## Seven-day pilot

### Day 0: qualify

Ask how leads arrive, where they are tracked, what gets missed, who follows up, and what
report they already use. Record the current baseline without pitching features.

### Day 1: configure

Create users, import only consented lead data, set owners, confirm stages, and schedule the
first follow-ups. Keep WhatsApp in mock mode unless the business owns and approves the Meta
credentials.

### Days 2-5: observe

Check whether the user logs in without prompting, completes follow-ups, moves stages, and
uses the Sheets report. Keep a dated issue log. Fix the smallest high-frequency friction.

### Day 6: interview

Ask: What would you miss if this disappeared? What remained manual? Which screen did you
avoid? What result improved? Capture quotes only with permission.

### Day 7: evidence

Update `REAL_USER_VALIDATION.md`, the pilot tracker, screenshots, usage counts, changes made,
and the next decision. Never label a demo account as a user.

## Launch metrics

- Activated: created/imported 5 leads and scheduled 1 follow-up.
- Retained: returned on 3 separate days in a week.
- Workflow value: completed 5 follow-ups or recovered 1 overdue lead.
- Reporting value: opened or refreshed the Sheets dashboard twice.
- Product learning: at least one shipped change tied to observed feedback.
