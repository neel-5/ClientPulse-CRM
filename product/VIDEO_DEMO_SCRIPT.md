# Two-Minute Video Demo Script

## 0:00-0:15 — Problem

“Small teams receive leads on WhatsApp, track follow-ups in memory, and rebuild reports in
Google Sheets. ClientPulse gives them one operating system for the conversation, next
action, pipeline, and reporting.”

Show the login and overview dashboard.

## 0:15-0:55 — Live product walkthrough

Receive a mock WhatsApp message. Show that it creates or finds the lead and stores message
history. Generate an AI reply draft, review it, move the lead to `Interested`, and schedule
a follow-up. Briefly show the follow-up agenda and kanban.

## 0:55-1:15 — Sheets and automation

Run Sheets sync. Show the pivot-ready template, validation lists, dashboard formulas, and
Apps Script custom menu. Open one Python automation script and the automation log screen.

## 1:15-1:30 — GitHub proof

Open `github.com/neel-5/ClientPulse-CRM`. Show the public commit history, tests, architecture
document, environment template, and provider adapters.

## 1:30-1:45 — Real use-case proof

After a genuine pilot, show consented, blurred evidence and the completed
`REAL_USER_VALIDATION.md`. State the business problem, usage frequency, and one change made
from feedback. Until then, say clearly that the product is pilot-ready and demo data is not
a customer claim.

## 1:45-2:00 — What broke, decisions, learning

“The first challenge was making provider credentials optional without creating fake
screens. I built persistent mock adapters that exercise the same workflows, then added
environment-driven live modes. The project taught me to design for user proof, operational
logs, and graceful failure, not only feature completion.”
