# Product Roadmap

## Now: pilot-ready

- Shared WhatsApp lead inbox in mock/live modes.
- Lead table, kanban pipeline, notes, tasks, and follow-ups.
- Google Sheets API/CSV export and Apps Script dashboard.
- Local AI scoring, reply drafts, summaries, next action, and risk notes.
- Role-based access, activity logs, automation logs, tests, and Docker support.

## Next: first five real users

- Workspace-level onboarding wizard.
- Custom fields and pipeline stages.
- Bulk CSV import with field mapping.
- Round-robin assignment and owner notifications.
- Contact consent and opt-out flags.
- Real AI provider adapter with usage controls.
- Error monitoring and product analytics.

## Later: repeatable SaaS

- Multi-tenant organizations and subscription plans.
- Meta embedded signup and per-tenant credentials.
- Campaign templates with policy-safe scheduling.
- Two-way Google Sheets change sync.
- SLA reporting, attribution, and forecast dashboards.
- Mobile-first PWA and richer calendar integrations.

Roadmap decisions should be driven by recorded pilot evidence, not feature count.
